A Math Study Tool For Students

I've created this website as a personal tool for studying my course materials as well as computer science on the side. The decision to make this project public was to create a resource for other students that may also be in need of help. Different resources will be posted here from my course notes, teachers' course notes, publicly available textbooks. Hopefully you all can make the most of this. There will be a way to contact me below in order to ask questions, make requests for material and or provide your own to be added to the site. The more the merrier. Thank you!

Preview of some courses:

  • MAT2125: Elementary Real Analysis

    A rigorous course covering the definitons and inner workings of sequences, series, topology, continuity and more. A strong ability to create proofs is needed for this course.

  • MAT1362: Mathematical Reasoning and Proofs

    A course that breaks down the fundamentals of math, proven through various proof techniques, covering concepts from geometry, algebra, number theory and more ...
