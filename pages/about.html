Just Anoter Math Student

I'm just any other math student but personally require diving deeper into tangents in order to better understand the material presented in classes. This means that I will not only be posting information about courses I am or have been enrolled in, but will also be covering material that I beleive are good parrallels to the my courses taken. Different texbooks provide different information and various ways of thinking. That is why I suggest that anyone studying mathematics also consider textbooks outside their direct requirements.

My Notes

My notes will present my personal understanding of certain concepts. There may be errors throughout so I would invite feedback in order to: better understand the material, fix my mistakes and provide a better experience for the users of my site

Future of this website

I'm not sure what will come of this website but I hope we can all improve as a result of its creation. My hope is to continuously develop new tools such as custom calculators with free step by step instructions, an exercise bank with steps, a way to connect students with tutors and more. Again, suggestions for features will always be welcome and any help with development will be appreciated.

Final Remark

Please understand that this website will have bugs, there may be mistakes in my notes, teachers notes, textbooks and more that I can't predict. Thank you for checking out my site.
